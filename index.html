QPath (need to be on VCU’s network)

Platform for managing ultrasound studies. After you finish scanning, images are uploaded to QPath. Then you log into QPath and interpret them. Then they get reviewed/approved by the ultrasound team.

You can access a hyperlink to QPath under “links” tool in Epic toolbar. Sign in with VCU health email.

EDRS & PDMP

Electronic Death Records System; this is the form you fill out to tell the state your patient died :(

PDMP is the state’s prescription drug monitoring program. You’ll be prompted to review the PDMP before you prescribe narcotics, then you have to authenticate the prescription by approving a notification from an app called “Imprivata” on your work phone.

GME can help you get your login credentials/set up for both of these.
CPRS

Charting system used by the Veteran Affairs (VA). This is the link for remote login. You need a PIV card reader and your issued PIV card (government badge) to access. You probably won’t need to use this intern year (even during your anesthesia rotation at the VA).
PACS

View CT scans/MRIs obtained at outside hospitals. The outside hospital has to upload it to VCU’s image resource center.

Really only useful for patients that get directly transferred to us from outside hospitals. For other patients, theoretically you can try finding the contact info to the outside hospital in our radiology department's database and then call/request them to upload the images to our image center. (Or just repeat the MRI….)
  • Radiology department’s database for contacting outside hospitals (need to be on VCU’s network)
